About Our Mission

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading healthcare provider and combat one of humanity’s greatest enemies— death —through quality, accessible, and transformative healthcare solutions.


Our Mission

To be the benchmark for excellent and innovative healthcare practices across Africa, committed to improving livelihood of African communities through compassion, innovation, and community-driven services.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by three graduates with a passion for transforming African healthcare, AHI began as a small group. After a split in 2021, the mission endured, eventually leading to the registration of Amodas Mobiclinic in 2023.

Rebranded by a growing membership to the African Health Initiative (AHI), we now symbolize a renewed commitment to serve the entire continent. Today, we are community-powered and sustained by member contributions and donations from Africans worldwide.
